Day 2

Mastering the Bangalore Real Estate Market

Develop a deep understanding of the Bangalore real estate landscape.

Segment 1: Bangalore Connectivity Deep Dive

Efficient meeting planning requires a thorough knowledge of Bangalore's key arterial roads and Namma Metro lines. This allows you to bypass traffic and reach clients effectively.

Key Road Networks 🛣️

  • Outer Ring Road (ORR): A 60 km loop road that is the lifeline for the IT corridor, connecting major hubs like Marathahalli, HSR Layout, and Hebbal.
  • Bellary Road (NH 44): The primary route connecting the city center to Kempegowda International Airport and North Bangalore.
  • Sarjapur Road: A major IT and real estate corridor in East Bangalore, connecting the ORR to Sarjapur town.
  • NICE Ring Road: Connects Electronic City and Bannerghatta Road to the western parts of the city, offering a faster (toll-based) alternative.

Namma Metro Network 🚇

  • Purple Line: Runs East-West, connecting Whitefield (Kadugodi) to Challaghatta. Crucial for the IT crowd.
  • Green Line: Runs North-South, connecting Nagasandra to Silk Institute. Vital for industrial and residential areas.
  • Blue Line (Upcoming): A game-changer connecting Silk Board to the Airport via the ORR, set to drastically improve North-South connectivity.

Segment 3: The Channel Partner (CP) Profile

  • Who they are: A CP is a builder's official marketing partner who sells properties for a brokerage fee. They are our primary client.
  • Their Goal: To get a consistent flow of high-quality leads to convert into site visits and sales.
  • The POC: The owner is your main Point of Contact. Approach them as a consultant, not a salesperson.
Day 3

Sales Pitch & Calling

Learn the pitch, practice scripts, and make live calls.

Training Focus

Learn the DigitalBuzz cold calling pitch and scripts. Focus on the "Straight Line Method" to establish authority in the first 30 seconds.

Real Calling

Supervised live calls to potential clients to practice the pitch and build confidence under the guidance of senior team members.

Day 4 & 5

F2F Meeting Module Training

A detailed walkthrough of the face-to-face (F2F) meeting process.

This includes pre-meeting research, structuring the conversation from greeting to strategy presentation, and discussing company policies. You'll learn how to present our value proposition effectively and confidently.

Day 6

Mock Sessions

Practice F2F meetings in a controlled environment.

Conduct mock F2F meetings with trainers and senior RMs playing the role of the client. Receive constructive feedback on your presentation, communication, and objection handling skills.

Day 7, 8, & 9

On-Field Meetings & Objection Handling

Gain real-world experience by shadowing senior team members.

Accompany senior RMs on actual client meetings. Observe how they build rapport, present solutions, and handle real-world objections. You will participate in post-meeting debriefs to analyze the interactions.

Day 10

Your First Meeting (Shadowed)

Lead your first client meeting with support.

You will lead your own client meeting from start to finish. A senior RM or Team Lead will accompany you to observe and provide support if needed. This is your chance to apply what you've learned.

Day 11

Independent Meetings

Conduct client meetings on your own.

You will conduct your own client meetings independently. The focus is on applying all the training from the previous days in a live environment and taking full ownership of the client interaction.

Day 12

Review Meeting

A comprehensive review of your training performance and setting future goals.

Agenda

  • Discuss feedback from mock calls and on-field meetings.
  • Review your understanding of products and processes.
  • Address any remaining questions or challenges.
  • Set clear performance goals for your first 30 days as a qualified Relationship Manager.
